Frequently Asked Questions

What payments methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards(Visa, MasterCard,
American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are processed to protext
your information.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on location and the shipping method selected. Typically
orders are processed within 1-2 business days, with standard shipping taking 5-7
business days.

Can i return an item?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please contact our support
system if you'd like to initiate a return, and we'll guide you through the process.

How do i track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number.
You can use this number to track the status of your shipment on our website or the
courier's website.
